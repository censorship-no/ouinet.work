Effective Date and Scope

This Privacy notice is effective as of December 1, 2018. In this privacy notice (Privacy notice), the terms “we”, “our” and “us” mean eQualit.ie. This Privacy notice governs our practices with respect to Data that we collect through the CENO browser.

About Ouinet

Ouinet is designed to provide you with open access to online censored content.

Interaction data

Ouinet stores a summary collection of all public web pages viewed by CENO users to enable other users of the system access to those pages. While we very much attempt to honor user privacy with this mechanism, it is still in early stages of development and it is possible private web pages viewed by users might be stored this way by accident.

Storage of Data Outside Canada

We store data outside of Canada.

Data Retention Policy

Web page summary collection items are retained indefinitely. Logs for testing purposes will be retained for the duration of the testing and analysis period, and will be deleted afterwards.

How Can You Access and Modify Your Personal Information or Make a Complaint?

Data stored in either the web page summary collection or the testing logs are not directly linked to a particular user. As a consequence, it is not possible to either access or modify information connected to a particular user. If you have questions or concerns, you can reach us at: privacy@equalit.ie
