Community

There are many ways to plug into the Ouinet community and the larger surrounding ecosystems!

Translations

Help translate the Ouinet documentation! We use Weblate to crowdsource translations.

Please contact the CENO team at ouinet [at] equalit [dot] ie (or the contact form below) if you're interested in becoming a reviewer for translation(s) in your language(s).

For more information about how to use Weblate, see the Weblate documentation.

Collaborations

The Ouinet team is curious and eager to build collaborative relationships with aligned groups and projects. Please get in touch via one of the following methods!

  • Email the team at cenoers[at] equalit [dot [ie]
  • Join our shared Matrix channel, Decentralized Wen Browsers
